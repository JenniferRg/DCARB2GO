Welcome to Decarb2GO®

A strategic force in the mission to eliminate gas flaring and foster sustainable energy solutions. Headquartered in Houston, we are dedicated to creating a brighter and cleaner future for generations to come.

Our main product

"Flare Free," is a groundbreaking solution to contribute the World Bank fantastic initiative denominated “Zero Routine Flaring by 2030”. Developed by our team of expert process engineers, this innovative technology in two main stages ensures not only the effective reduction of gas flaring but also sets the standard for environmentally responsible practices in the industry. It represents our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and a cleaner planet.

Our team comprises seasoned process engineers who bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the table. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our projects are not only environmentally but also proves economically viable, unlocking opportunities for resource optimization and sustainable energy practices.
